Bread of Life is a calm, offline-first "homebase" for your devotional life —
one place for reading, prayer, and reflection instead of five separate apps.

Features:

* Read the Berean Standard Bible (public domain), bundled for full offline use,
  plus WEB, KJV, ASV and YLT. Verse-per-line or flowing layout, highlights,
  notes, chapter audio, and a fast full-text search.
* A study rail with public-domain commentary, cross-references (Treasury of
  Scripture Knowledge), and Strong's word-study — Greek for the NT and an
  accurate Hebrew interlinear (Westminster Leningrad Codex) for the OT.
* A prayer log at the heart of it: mark prayers answered and look back on what
  God has done. Daily reminders optional.
* A rich-text journal with tags, search, and one-tap capture from any verse.
* Selectable public-domain devotionals (Spurgeon's Morning & Evening and
  Faith's Checkbook) with a warm dashboard and reading plans.
* An optional AI study companion you bring your own key for (Claude, OpenAI,
  or a local model via Ollama).

Everything you write stays on your device. No account required.
