Bread of Life

A warm, offline-first home for your devotional life.

Read the Bible, keep a journal, and track your prayers — all stored on your own device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Just you and the Word, everywhere you go.

The heart of the app

An answered-prayer log you'll treasure

Write down what you're praying for. When the answer comes, mark it answered and add a note on how God moved. Nothing gets lost, nothing gets forgotten.

Then look back. Scroll through months and years of faithfulness written in your own words — a living record of what God has done.
